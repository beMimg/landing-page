Feeling on the wheels
When driving a car, you can choose where they want to go and how fast want to get there. This can provide a feeling of power and independence, which may be appealing to those who feel like they have little control over their lives.
What's your type?
A convertible is a car with a retractable roof that can be opened to convert it from a closed, enclosed vehicle to an open-air one, popular among drivers who enjoy the feeling of the wind in their hair and the open road ahead.
A coupe is a two-door car with a fixed roof, typically designed with a sloping roofline that extends towards the rear of the vehicle. It's often seen as a sporty and stylish option, and can range from compact models to high-end luxury vehicles.
A hatchback is a car body style that features a rear door or tailgate that opens upwards and includes both the rear window and trunk area. This design allows for more flexibility in terms of cargo space and passenger seating arrangements, making it a popular choice for small families or individuals who prioritize practicality.
A SUV (Sport Utility Vehicle) is a large, versatile vehicle that is designed for both on-road and off-road use. It typically features a high ground clearance, four-wheel drive, and ample passenger and cargo space, making it a popular choice for families, outdoor enthusiasts, and those who need to navigate challenging terrain.
